ABOUT US

The contemporary Indian beauty expert - Glamour Crate continuously innovates to offer a wide range of high performance and world class cosmetics and skincare products that have earned recognition from the People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA). Glamour Crate was the country's first cosmetic brand to introduce makeup to Indian women and takes pride in being the expert on Indian Beauty for over 50 years.
